MAU Game

MAU is an endless runner game on Telegram where players navigate obstacles to achieve the highest XP among fellow felines. This play-to-earn (P2E) adventure features leaderboards, tournament modes, and a referral system that rewards players with MAU tokens for both playing and inviting friends. Enhance your gameplay by purchasing XP boosts, extra lives, and checkpoints through MAU’s Web3 payment solutions.

Play-to-Earn and Refer-to-Earn


* Earn XP every time you play MAU


* Gain additional XP by referring other felines to join the game


* The more you play and refer, the more XP and tokens you earn!


* XP is converted into MAU tokens!
